Physical Layer
==============

Talk about the different types of transmission mediums:

* Wire (guided)
* Light (guided or possibly unguided)
* Radio (unguided)

Wire:

* Regular wire
* Twisted Pair - TP, Shielded or unshielded
* Coax

Light:

* IR (like older TV remotes)
* Laser + fiber optics

Radio:

* Bluetooth
* Wi-Fi
* Microwave links
* Satellite

  * Low Earth Orbit
  * Geosynchronous
